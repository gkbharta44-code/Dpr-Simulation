Channel shares (consumer supply chain) hold 6 months of turnover in advance; users hold 1 month; commodity & derivative trading participants hold 1 month against DPR-settled commodity volume (70% of $140T/yr → $98T/yr). Token counts recalculate every tick against the live simulated market price. Whenever the combined full-hold requirement would exceed the locked 498B cap, every party's hold period is scaled down by the same accommodation factor — shown in the KPI strip above — until price recovers toward its market-cap anchor.
Illustrative simulation only — figures are synthetic and generated live in-browser to demonstrate the mechanism described in the DPR Tokenomics Whitepaper v2.0 (Sections 6, 7 & 10). Token value is anchored to ecosystem market capitalization — dominated by a targeted 70% capture of the ~$140T global commodity & derivative trading market — divided by the 1.998T total token supply, with the USD 21.4 floor as an absolute downside backstop. Price floor is defended manually by GK Bharta, drawing on the GK Bharta Reserve, which receives only the floor-equivalent share of purchase proceeds; the above-floor premium on every purchase now flows to the liquidity pool instead, alongside the 0.5%+0.5% tax. Nothing here reflects real transaction data.
